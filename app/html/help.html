What is Art Map Generator ?


Art Map Generator is a software that helps you make MapArt by processing, converting a given picture and providing the right blocks,items and materials needed to create it in your Minecraft World

How to use Art Map Generator ?


1. Choosing an image


The chosen image must be png , jpg or jpeg.
You can choose the image by dragging it into the drag area or by clicking on the button and using the file dialog.

2. You want to use the coordinations?


The goal of the coordinates is to simplify the process of creating your MapArt by indicating where each block needs to be placed
The coordinates must be compatible with the type of chunk you choose for your MapArt , for example if you choose the 128x128 type ,

You can use Art map by dragging a picture you want to convert to the selected area or choosing it from the files in your computer and then click on the generate button.
the first coordinates must be equal to the second coordinates minus 127
